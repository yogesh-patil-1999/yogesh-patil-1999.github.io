Pig Game Project

About Pig Game 😍

“The pig game project” is basically a game to play in your idle time along with your friend or your partner. In this game you roll a dice and roll as many times as you don’t get 1 on dice. If you get 1 on dice then it’s your partner’s turn. You can hold a score and add it to total score if you want. If you get 1, your current score will be vanished and won’t be added to your total score. The winner is one who manages to get total score of 100 very first. 🎲🎮
